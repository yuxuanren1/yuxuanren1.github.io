I develop and apply an offline aerosol mass spectrometry (AMS) method to characterize organic aerosol on PTFE filters collected by the globally distributed Surface Particulate Matter Network ([SPARTAN](https://www.spartan-network.org/)), enabling organic aerosol characterization at sites without online instrumentation. I am also building a global organic aerosol dataset for evaluating the chemical transport model [GEOS-Chem](https://geoschem.github.io/index.html).
