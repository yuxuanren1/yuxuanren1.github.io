I evaluate global black carbon emissions by comparing the chemical transport model [GEOS-Chem](https://geoschem.github.io/index.html) with ground-based measurements from the globally distributed Surface Particulate Matter Network ([SPARTAN](https://www.spartan-network.org/)). This work showed that widely used emission inventories systematically underestimate black carbon in the Global South, with implications for air quality policy and health burden estimates.
